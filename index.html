About us

Founded in 2025, Asanka Restaurant is a West African family-style restaurant based in Toronto, serving authentic dishes from Ghana, Nigeria, and beyond. Named after the traditional Ghanaian clay bowl, Asanka is a warm, inclusive space for food lovers of all backgrounds—young or old, professionals or students, families or individuals.
We blend rich flavors with vibrant experiences like karaoke nights and the spicy Asanka K3se3 food challenge. Whether you're in the diaspora or simply craving a taste of West Africa, come for the food, stay for the fun.
Asanka Restaurant – A Taste of Home, A Bowl of Joy.
